Rules and Guidelines for Administrators

1. Eligibility

Only authorized individuals with proper credentials are eligible to access the administration panel of the online voting system. Admins should have a thorough understanding of the system and its functionality.

2. Security

Administrators must ensure the security and integrity of the voting system. This includes implementing strong authentication mechanisms, regularly updating software and security patches, and safeguarding sensitive voter data.

3. Confidentiality

Admins should handle voter data with the utmost confidentiality. They must adhere to strict privacy guidelines and protect the personal information of voters from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Impartiality

Administrators must maintain impartiality and ensure a fair and unbiased electoral process. They should not engage in any activities that could influence the outcome of the elections or show favoritism towards any candidate or party.

5. Transparency

Admins should ensure transparency in the administration of the online voting system. They should provide clear and accurate information to voters, address any concerns or queries promptly, and maintain open lines of communication.
